Chase Bliss Audio Clean

The Chase Bliss Audio Clean is a fully analog studio compressor pedal with sound-designer-grade controls (Dynamics, Sensitivity, Wet, Attack, EQ, Dry, Release, Mode, Physics). It is widely used as a parallel-compression tool by mix engineers who want pedalboard-friendly access to studio-grade dynamics.

Roles: Guitarist, Music Producer

"This is hands-down the most musical compressor I've ever put on my pedalboard — it's like a studio-grade 1176 you can squish in front of your amp.
